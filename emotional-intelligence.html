Intelligence Quotient

IQ is a measure of how clever you are, how well you solve problems, understand ideas and thinking logically.

Pro's

  • Higher IQ can lead to better grades
  • Higher Iq can lead to better job opportunies
  • Helps you figure things out quickly

Con's

  • IQ doesnt measure a persons creativity

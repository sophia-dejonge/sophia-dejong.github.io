Understanding Intelligence & Emotional Quotient

Intelligence Quotient

IQ is a measure of how clever you are, how well you solve problems, understand ideas and thinking logically.

Pro's

  • Higher IQ can lead to better grades
  • Higher Iq can lead to better job opportunies
  • Helps you figure things out quickly

Con's

  • IQ doesnt measure a persons creativity
  • Or their ability to emotionally understand
  • Or any practical skills one might have
  • Tests also do not accomodate to the cultural differences making it quite bias

Emotional Quotient

EQ is all about how well you understand and manage emotions, both your own and others

Pro's

  • Knowing how you feel.
  • Handling your emotions in a healthy way.
  • Using your emotions to stay focused and positive.
  • Understanding how others feel.
  • Social Skills: Getting along with people and handling social situations.

Con's

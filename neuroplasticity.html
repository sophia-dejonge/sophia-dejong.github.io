Growth Mindset

      What it is and why it's relevant?
        -A growth mindset is not being afraid of change. 
        It's the belief that you can get better and smarter at things through learning and effort. 
        It's important to have a growth mindset because it helps
        you learn from mistakes , try new challenging things and you just keep improving . 
        Instead of having a fixed mindset where you aren't willing to try or do anything new .
